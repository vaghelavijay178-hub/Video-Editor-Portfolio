About Me

Crafting stories frame by frame.

I was making graphite and charcoal portraits and filming them. From this I got the chance to work professionally as a Video Editor — and I find it genuinely interesting.

I've honed my skills over 1.5+ years, and that's my starting point. Since 2023 I've never lost interest in creating attention-grabbing, storytelling videos.

I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects — from faceless videos to vertical vlogs, fashion content, and brand videos.
